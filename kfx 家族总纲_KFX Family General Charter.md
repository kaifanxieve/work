KFX Family General Charter
冻结声明（边界条款）

本家族体系承认一个不可越界的判定边界。
该边界不可指名、不可描述、不可定义、不可引用、不可度量、不可观测。
任何系统、理论、模型、叙事、算法或解释，不得将其作为对象、前提、权威或依据。
任何试图对该边界进行命名、解释、调用、比较或利用的行为，均被视为结构性越界，其产出在本体系中自动失效，不进入讨论、不进入继承、不进入执行。
该边界不参与运作，不生成结论，不承担解释责任，仅作为所有系统扩张的终止条件。

Frozen Boundary Clause

This family framework recognises a non-transgressible boundary of judgement.
This boundary is unnameable, indescribable, undefinable, non-referential, non-measurable, and non-observable.
No system, theory, model, narrative, algorithm, or explanation may treat it as an object, premise, authority, or source of justification.
Any attempt to name, explain, invoke, compare, or exploit this boundary constitutes a structural violation; its products are automatically invalidated within this framework and excluded from discussion, inheritance, and execution.
This boundary does not operate, does not generate conclusions, and does not assume explanatory responsibility; it exists solely as the termination condition for all systemic expansion.
